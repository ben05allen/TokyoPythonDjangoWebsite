Who We Are

About Us.

Tokyo Python is a vibrant, English-speaking community for Python enthusiasts based in Tokyo. Founded in 2017, we bring together developers of all levels — from beginners writing their first script to seasoned engineers building production systems.

Whether you're looking to learn, share knowledge, or just meet fellow Pythonistas, you'll find a welcoming home with us.
